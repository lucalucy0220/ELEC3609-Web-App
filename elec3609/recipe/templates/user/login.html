We are more than just a recipe website.

Our solution is to create a community post-driven website where people can submit and post their own recipes, almost like a ‘specialised Pinterest’ in terms of purpose. But why should people use our site? Our aim is to foster an increased sense of community, which we feel is incredibly important especially during this pandemic, while people are isolated and in need of human connection. We believe we can do this by having a website dedicated to a common interest, as well as encouraging users to post their ideas and interact with other users via commenting, following or sending DMs. All of these features mentioned will hopefully will help us achieve our goal.
